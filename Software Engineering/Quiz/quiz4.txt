1.Which of the following best describes software project management?
A.Writing program code efficiently
B.Ensuring software is delivered on time, within budget, and meets requirements
C.Testing software for bugs
D.Designing system architecture
Answer: B

2.What is a key challenge in software project management? (Slide: Software management distinctions)
A.Software is tangible
B.Software projects are repetitive
C.Progress is not directly visible
D.Requirements never change
Answer: C

3.Which is NOT a success criterion of software project management? (Slide: Success criteria)
A.Deliver on time
B.Stay within budget
C.Maximize developer workload
D.Meet customer expectations
Answer: C

4.Which activity involves identifying and mitigating risks? (Slide: Project management activities)
A.Reporting
B.Risk management
C.Proposal writing
D.Scheduling

5.Which type of risk affects software quality? (Slide: Risk classification)
A.Project risk
B.Product risk

6.What is the first step in the risk management process? (Slide: Risk management process)

7.Which strategy reduces the probability of a risk occurring? (Slide: Risk planning)
