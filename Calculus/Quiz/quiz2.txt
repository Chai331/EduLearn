1. Without using a calculator, find the exact value of sin(30°).
A. 1/2
B. sqrt(2)/2
C. sqrt(3)/2
D. 1
Answer: A

2. If cos(x) = 3/5 and x is in the first quadrant, what is the value of sin(x)?
A. 2/5
B. 4/5
C. 3/4
D. 1/5
Answer: B

3. In a right triangle ABC with the right angle at C, if side c = 10 and angle A = 30°, find the length of side a (opposite to angle A).
A. 5
B. 5sqrt(3)
C. 10
D. 20
Answer: A

4. Use the Law of Cosines to find the length of side a if b = 5, c = 8, and angle A = 60°.
A. 7
B. sqrt(129)
C. 9
D. sqrt(39)
Answer: A

5. Solve the exponential equation for x: 3^(x+1) = 27.
A. x = 1
B. x = 2
C. x = 3
D. x = 4
Answer: B

6. You deposit $1500 in an account that pays 5% interest compounded yearly. Which formula represents the balance after 6 years?
A. 1500(1 + 0.05)^6
B. 1500(0.05)^6
C. 1500 + (0.05 × 6)
D. 1500(1 + 5)^6
Answer: A

7. A mice population of 25,000 is decreasing by 20% each year. What is the population after 3 years?
A. 10,000
B. 12,800
C. 15,000
D. 20,000
Answer: B

8. Which of the following is the correct double-angle formula for sin(2x)?
A. sin^2 x − cos^2 x
B. 2 sin x cos x
C. 2 sin x
D. sin x + cos x
Answer: B

9. If interest is compounded continuously, which formula is used to find the balance A after t years?
A. A = P(1 + r/m)^(mt)
B. A = Pe^(rt)
C. A = P(1 + r)^t
D. A = Prt
Answer: B

10. Find the equation of the line that passes through the point (1, -1) with a slope of 3.
A. y = 3x - 4
B. y = 3x + 1
C. y = 3x - 1
D. y = 3x + 4
Answer: A
