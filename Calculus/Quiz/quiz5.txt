1.Given the function f(x) = 3 - 5x - 2x^2, what is the value of f(4)
A. -49
B. -45
C. 55
D. 3
Answer: A
2.The difference quotient of a function f(x) is defined as (f(x+h) - f(x)) / h. If f(x) = 2x + 1, what is its difference quotient
A. 1
B. 2
C. 2h
D. 2x
Answer: B
3.Which of the following is the correct domain for the function f(x) = 1 / (x - 5)
A. x > 5
B. x < 5
C. x != 5
D. All real numbers
Answer: C
4.If f(x) = x^2 and g(x) = x - 3, find the composite function (f ∘ g)(x)
A. x^2 - 3
B. x^2 - 9
C. x^2 - 6x + 9
D. x^2 + 6x + 9
Answer: C
5. Determine the parity of the function f(x) = x^2 + 4
A. Odd function
B. Even function
C. Neither even nor odd
D. Both even and odd
Answer: B
6.If y = x^2 + 3x - 1, find the derivative dy/dx
A. 2x
B. 2x + 3
C. x + 3
D. 2x - 1
Answer: B
7.Find the slope of the tangent line to the curve y = f(x) at a given point
A. f(x)
B. The integral of f(x)
C. The derivative f'(x) at that point
D. The value of x at the y-intercept
Answer: C
8.According to the Product Rule, the derivative of f(x) * g(x) is
A. f'(x) * g'(x)
B. f'(x) + g'(x)
C. f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x)
D. f(x)g(x) + f'(x)g'(x)
Answer: C
9.What is the derivative of sin(x)
A. cos(x)
B. -cos(x)
C. sin(x)
D. -sin(x)
Answer: A
10.When a function is defined by an equation such as x^2 + y^2 = 25, which method is used to find dy/dx
A. Power Rule only
B. Implicit differentiation
C. Quotient Rule only
